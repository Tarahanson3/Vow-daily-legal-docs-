Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

VowDaily is built on trust. Your marriage is deeply personal, and we treat your data with the care it deserves. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.
01

Our Commitment

We built VowDaily to help couples grow closer through daily prayer and intentional action. That mission requires your trust, and we take it seriously. We collect only what we need, protect what you share, and will never sell your personal data to anyone for any reason.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use VowDaily:

  • Your email address (for authentication)
  • A unique user identifier (generated automatically for your account)
  • Your name and your spouse's name
  • Anniversary and birthday dates (optional)
  • Faith tradition preference
  • Love language quiz results
  • Daily completion records and streak data
  • Check-in notes and reflections you write
  • Scheduled date nights
  • Photos you upload to day records (premium only)
  • Custom date night ideas you create
  • Notification preferences and reminder time
  • Purchase and subscription history (processed by RevenueCat)
We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We do not use your data for any purpose other than providing and improving the VowDaily service.
03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Personalize daily prayers, actions, and reflections for your spouse
  • Send local push notifications for daily reminders (if enabled)
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Generate AI-tailored content and label it as AI-generated (premium only)
  • Track streaks and display your marriage history
  • Process subscription payments through RevenueCat
  • Respond to support requests you submit
04

AI Content Generation and Disclosure

For premium users, we use OpenRouter to generate personalized devotional content. In compliance with FTC guidelines, all AI-generated content is clearly labeled as such within the app at the point where it is displayed to you.

To enable meaningful personalization, we send the following information to the AI model:

  • Your name and your spouse's name
  • Your spouse's love language
  • Your faith tradition
  • Length of your marriage
  • Children's names and ages (if provided)
  • Pet names (if provided)
  • General themes from recent check-in notes and reflection answers
  • Summaries of previously generated content to avoid repetition
We send only what is necessary to generate meaningful personalized content. We do not sell this data, use it for advertising, or share it beyond OpenRouter for the sole purpose of content generation. Generated content is cached server-side. AI-generated content is intended as spiritual inspiration and should not replace professional counseling or pastoral guidance.
05

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Supabase (a PostgreSQL cloud database)
  • Authentication credentials are managed by Supabase Auth
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • We use Row Level Security so each user can only access their own data
  • No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting tools are installed in the app
06

Third-Party Services

We work with the following trusted providers. Each has its own privacy policy and we share only the minimum information necessary for each service to function.

Supabase — Auth & Database RevenueCat — Subscriptions OpenRouter — AI Content (Premium) Apple App Store — Payments Google Play — Payments

We do not use any advertising networks, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics services. If this changes in the future we will update this policy and notify you in advance.

07

Your Content

You own the content you create in VowDaily — including your check-in notes, reflection answers, custom date night ideas, and photos. VowDaily stores this content solely to provide the service back to you. We do not claim ownership of your personal content and we do not use it for any purpose beyond delivering your personalized experience within the app.

08

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data — including your profile, day records, check-ins, reflections, photos, and scheduled dates — at any time from Settings. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 30 days of discovering the breach via the email address associated with your account.
09

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

VowDaily is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 17. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 17 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@vowdaily.app and we will delete that information promptly.

10

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information.

We do not sell your personal information. California residents may exercise their rights by contacting us at support@vowdaily.app or by deleting their account directly in the app Settings.
11

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Use the in-app Settings to update your profile or delete your account. For additional data requests, contact us at support@vowdaily.app and we will respond within 30 days.

12

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the app before the changes take effect. Continued use of VowDaily after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
