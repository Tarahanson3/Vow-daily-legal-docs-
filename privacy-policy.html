Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

VowDaily is built on trust. Your marriage is deeply personal, and we treat your data with the care it deserves. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.
01

Our Commitment

We built VowDaily to help couples grow closer through daily prayer and intentional action. That mission requires your trust, and we take it seriously. We collect only what we need, protect what you share, and never sell your data.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use VowDaily:

  • Your email address (for authentication)
  • Your name and your spouse's name
  • Anniversary and birthday dates (optional)
  • Faith tradition preference
  • Love language quiz results
  • Daily completion records
  • Check-in notes and reflections
  • Scheduled date nights
  • Photos attached to day records (premium only)
  • Notification preferences
03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Personalize daily prayers, actions, and reflections for your spouse
  • Send local push notifications for daily reminders (if enabled)
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Generate AI-tailored content (premium only)
  • Track streaks and display your history
04

AI Content Generation

For premium users, we use OpenRouter to generate personalized devotional content.

What we send to the AI: your spouse's love language and preferred tone.

What we do NOT send: your spouse's name, personal details, or check-in notes. Generated content is cached server-side.
05

Data Storage

  • Your data is stored securely in Supabase (a PostgreSQL cloud database)
  • Authentication credentials are managed by Supabase Auth
  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • We use Row Level Security so each user can only access their own data
06

Third-Party Services

We work with the following trusted providers. Each has its own privacy policy and we share only the minimum information necessary.

Supabase — Auth & Database RevenueCat — Subscriptions OpenRouter — AI Content (Premium) Apple App Store — Payments Google Play — Payments
07

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data — including your profile, day records, check-ins, reflections, photos, and scheduled dates — at any time from Settings. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

08

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Use the in-app Settings to update your profile or delete your account. For additional requests, contact us directly.
